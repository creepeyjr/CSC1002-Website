Market & Customers

Our product is aimed at beginners who are only getting started in the gym, elderly people who are trying to maintain mobility and strength and amateur gym goers who want personal training and feedback, but can't afford to hire a professional trainer.


We went about identifying these people by creating a simple survey that interviewed a variety of people in the gym and found these people to be common outliers. They expressed frustration with their level and rate of progress in the gym, which helped us decide who our target market would be.


The fitness tech industry is booming, with the global fitness app market projected to grow from $15 billion in 2022 to $34.4 billion by 2027. With increasing demand for AI-driven solutions, Form X is well-positioned to capitalize on this growth by offering an innovative, data-driven approach to fitness.


We believe Form X is not just an app—it's a fitness companion, empowering users to train smarter, move better, and achieve their goals with confidence.

Future Market Trends

The fitness industry is evolving and growing each year. As new fitness practices and trends make way, consumers gravitate toward fitness models that best align with their goals and schedules. With consumers entering 2023 with a focus on fitness, there has been a rise in health and wellness investments. According to the International Health, Racquet, & Sportsclub Association (IHRSA), a fitness industry analysis shows that for the past ten years, the $87 billion health and fitness industry has been growing by 3-4% annually.


This rise is due to new trends in the industry, like boutique fitness studios, fitness franchises, online fitness classes, and studios that offer a community-oriented experience. We expect these trends to stay on the rise and to continue to grow interest in consumers, as it allows for a more personalized approach to fitness.


This is where our app comes in to greater evolve a ever-growing market. Our app provides an affordable, intelligent, and adaptable approach to fitness. By aligning with evolving consumer trends, FormX is not just keeping up with the fitness industry—it’s shaping its future.

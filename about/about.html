About Us

Form X is a mobile application, where users record themselves performing an exercise, and utilising computer vision and our AI, the app gives real time feedback on form, posture and technique.

Our Mission

We aim to make training feel more rewarding. By offering instant, intelligent corrections, we help users build better habits, reduce injury risk, and train with more confidence.

Our Vision

To provide a digital alternative to a personal trainer, helping users build confidence and clarity in their fitness journey. We want to make good form, guidance, and encouragement accessible — no matter where or when you're training.

Our Team

We’re a dedicated team of professionals with diverse backgrounds in technology, fitness, and design. What brings us together is a shared passion for building tools that make a real difference. With expertise in AI, computer vision, and user experience, we’ve come together to create Form X.

For Our Clients

What is Form X?

Form X is a mobile app that uses AI and computer vision to provide real-time feedback on posture and exercise technique. Users simply record themselves and receive instant guidance to improve safely and effectively.

The Problem We’re Solving

Many people want to get fit but don’t know where to start. Beginners often feel overwhelmed or lack proper guidance. Form X offers a clear, interactive solution — combining real-time workout feedback, AI coaching, and long-term progress tracking.

How It Works

Our app uses your phone’s camera to analyze movement and joint angles. Our AI compares this to correct form and gives users immediate tips to adjust. No extra equipment or expertise needed.

Who It’s For

- First-time gym users - Elderly users focusing on mobility - Anyone who wants a digital personal trainer

We built Form X after talking to real people in real gyms. We heard frustration, confusion, and a desire for better support. This product is our response.
